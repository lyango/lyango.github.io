Project: Boom Bot

Boom Bot is a information based bot developed for Discord users who play Riot Games' Valorant. The bot actively takes data from playvalorant.com and will concurrently, and automatically update based off Riot Games. With this, the bot will always be up-to-date with Valorant agents, maps, and arsenal automatically without need any manual updates.

Utilizing Javascript, Node.js, and Discord development tools, users can input commands on servers that Boom Bot is deployed in. Boom Bot can show information about every agent, their abilities, playable maps, and each weapon that can be used in the game.
