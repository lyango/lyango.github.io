> Project: BOOM BOT 

Boom Bot is a information based bot developed for Discord users that are players of Riot Games' Valorant. The bot actively takes data from playvalorant.com and will concurrently, and automatically update based off Riot Games. With this, the bot will always be up-to-date with Valorant's agents, maps, and arsenal automatically without needing to update.

Utilizing Node.js and Discord development tools, user can input commands on servers that Boom Bot is deployed in. Boom Bot can show information about every agent, their abilities, playable maps, and each weapon that can be used in the game.
