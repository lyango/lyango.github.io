Project: Glue Bot Galaxy

Game was created in 72 hours for one of the biggest Game Jams globally, Ludum Dare 50, with the theme: "delay the inevitable". The game was created with a team of 5 people, spanning from art, music, and programming. I was 1 of the 2 programmers on the team, and led development over the course of the 72 hours for gameplay mechanics.

The game was created with C# and Unity Game Engine, and is a 3D third/first person "shooter" where you spray glue to stick back the cracking pieces of the planet from falling apart. I programmed the gravity logic for the player to walk across the planet, player mechanics, as well as other game logic.
