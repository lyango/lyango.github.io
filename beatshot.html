Project: Beat Shot

This 3D rhythm shooter was created in 7 days for the 7DFPS 2022 Game Jam. The game was created with only 2 people including myself. Being the only programmer on the team, I focused development over the course of the 7 days on gameplay mechanics including allowing the player to shoot on a specific beat of a song; this system allowed me to extend this to other aspects of the game such as animating game objects based on the beat. Additionally, I worked on player movement, a state machine based enemy AI, game systems, and level design.
